The Artist

Made With Intention

Each painting begins as an intention — whether the radial precision of a mandala or the vibrant storytelling of folk art. Every piece is created entirely by hand, layer by layer, using fine brushes and an instinct shaped by years of practice.

No two are ever alike. Whether you're drawn to the meditative calm of cool blues or the energy of warm ochres, you'll find something here that speaks to you.

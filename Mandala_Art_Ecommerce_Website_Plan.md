## 2. Gallery / Shop Page

### Categorisation Options

| Category Type | Examples |
|--------------|----------|
| By Collection | Sacred Geometry, Floral Mandalas, Celestial Series, Earth Tones |
| By Size | Small (≤20cm), Medium (21–50cm), Large (51cm+) |
| By Colour Palette | Warm, Cool, Monochrome, Multi-colour |
| By Price Range | Under £100, £100–£250, £250+ |

**Recommendation:** Use **Collections as primary** categories (they tell a story), with size and price as **filters**.

### Product Listing: Required Fields

- **Title** — e.g., *"Solstice — Original Mandala Painting"*
- **Size** — e.g., 40 × 40 cm
- **Medium** — e.g., Acrylic on stretched canvas
- **Price** — clearly displayed
- **Availability** — Original (1 of 1) / Limited print / Sold (keep sold works visible — it builds credibility)
- **Inspiration note** — 2–3 sentences on what inspired this piece
- **Shipping note** — e.g., *"Ships within 5–7 days, carefully packaged"*
- **Care instructions** — link or tooltip

### Image Display
- **3–5 images per listing:** full painting, close-up of detail, lifestyle shot (on a wall), size reference (painting next to a common object), and ideally a short process video or GIF
- Link each listing to its Instagram post: *"See the making of this piece →"*
- Use square or portrait crop consistently across all listings

### Filtering & Sorting
- Filter by: Collection, Size, Colour, Price Range, Availability
- Sort by: Newest, Price (low–high / high–low), Popular

- Years painting
- Favourite colour to work with

## 5. FAQ Page

### Suggested Questions & Answers

**Purchasing**
- *Are your paintings originals?* — Yes, each is a one-of-a-kind hand-painted original on canvas.
- *Do you offer prints?* — [Yes/No — your call to answer]
- *Can I commission a custom piece?* — Yes. Reach out via the contact form with your size, colour preferences, and any intention you'd like the piece to carry.

**Shipping**
- *Where do you ship?* — Worldwide.
- *How long does shipping take?* — Paintings ship within 5–7 days of purchase. Delivery varies by destination: UK (3–5 days), Europe (7–10), International (10–21).
- *How are paintings packaged?* — Each canvas is wrapped in acid-free tissue, bubble-wrapped, and boxed in a rigid outer box to prevent damage in transit.

**Returns & Care**
- *What is your returns policy?* — Originals are non-refundable (due to their unique nature), but I'll work with you if your painting arrives damaged.
- *How do I care for my painting?* — Keep out of direct sunlight. Dust gently with a dry soft cloth. Do not use water or cleaning products on the surface.

**Custom Orders**
- *How long does a custom piece take?* — Typically 3–6 weeks depending on size and complexity.
- *Do you require a deposit?* — Yes, a 50% deposit is required before work begins. Balance is due before shipping.

## 8. E-Commerce Functionality

### Essential Features

| Feature | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| Secure payment gateway | Stripe or PayPal are standard; both support international payments |
| Shopping cart | Single and multi-item checkout |
| Inventory management | Mark originals as "1 in stock" — auto-marks as sold when purchased |
| Shipping calculator | Integrate with Royal Mail / DHL / FedEx APIs or set flat rates by region |
| Order confirmation emails | Automated, with tracking info |
| Discount/coupon codes | For newsletter subscribers and returning customers |
| Wishlist / Save for later | Useful for higher-priced items where buyers take time to decide |
| SSL certificate | Mandatory for trust and SEO |

### Inventory Management for Originals
- Set each original as **quantity: 1**
- When sold, keep the listing visible but marked **"Sold — View Similar"** — this demonstrates demand and builds trust
- For prints (if you add them), use standard quantity tracking

## 9. Design & Aesthetics

### Colour Palette

```
Primary Background:   #FAF8F4  (warm off-white — lets art breathe)
Secondary:            #2C2C2C  (near-black for text — softer than pure black)
Accent 1:             #8B7355  (warm taupe — earthy, grounding)
Accent 2:             #C4956A  (terracotta — warm energy, highlights)
Accent 3:             #4A7C7E  (teal — calm, spiritual depth)
CTA Button:           #C4956A or #4A7C7E with white text
```

This palette is neutral enough to complement any painting without competing with it.

### Font Pairings

| Use | Font | Why |
|-----|------|-----|
| Headings | **Cormorant Garamond** (serif) | Elegant, artistic, slightly editorial |
| Body | **Inter** or **DM Sans** (sans-serif) | Clean, highly readable at all sizes |
| Accent/quotes | **Cormorant Garamond Italic** | Adds a poetic, personal touch |

Both are available free on Google Fonts.

### Layout Principles
- **Maximum content width:** 1200px — prevents text lines from becoming too long
- **Generous white space** around every painting — never crowd the art
- **No carousel abuse** — static featured grids outperform auto-sliding carousels
- **Mobile-first:** Over 60% of art browsing happens on phone; ensure paintings display large and tappable
- **Fast loading:** Compress images to WebP format without sacrificing quality (use Squoosh or similar)

## 10. CTA Strategy

| Location | CTA Text | Goal |
|----------|----------|------|
| Hero section | **"Explore the Collection"** | Drive to shop |
| Hero section (secondary) | **"Meet the Artist"** | Build trust |
| Featured paintings strip | **"View & Buy"** | Direct purchase intent |
| About page | **"See My Work"** | Cross-link to gallery |
| Blog posts | **"Find Your Painting"** | Convert content readers |
| Exit intent / footer | **"Join the Circle"** (newsletter) | Capture leads |
| Product page | **"Add to Cart"** + **"Ask a Question"** | Purchase or engage |
| Sold listings | **"Commission Something Similar"** | Capture lost sales |

**Rule of thumb:** Every page should have exactly one **primary CTA** and at most one **secondary CTA**. More than that creates decision paralysis.

## 12. Platform Recommendations

| Platform | Best For | Pricing (approx.) |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **Squarespace** | Artists wanting beautiful templates with minimal setup | £13–£23/mo |
| **Shopify** | Scalability, more products, serious e-commerce features | £25–£65/mo |
| **Big Cartel** | Very small catalogues, ultra-simple, artist-focused | Free–£15/mo |
| **Etsy** (+ own site) | Initial discoverability; run alongside your own domain | Listing fees + 6.5% |

**Recommendation:** Start on **Squarespace** (cleanest aesthetic, Instagram integration, good e-commerce) or **Shopify** if you plan to scale. Use Etsy in parallel early on for discoverability, then shift traffic to your own site over time.

## 13. Key SEO Considerations

- **Page titles:** Include keywords like *"original mandala painting"*, *"hand-painted canvas art"*, *"mandala wall art UK"*
- **Alt text on every image:** Describe the painting specifically — *"circular mandala painting in gold and teal, acrylic on canvas, 50cm"*
- **Blog content:** Targets long-tail searches like *"what do mandala colours mean"* or *"unique art gifts for meditation lovers"*
- **Local SEO (if relevant):** Add your city/region to your About page and metadata
- **Page speed:** Compress all images; use WebP format
- **Google Business Profile:** Create one even as an online-only business — helps with branded searches
- **Backlinks:** Submit to art directories, gift guides, and mindfulness blogs
- **Schema markup:** Use Product schema on listings so Google can display price/availability in search results
